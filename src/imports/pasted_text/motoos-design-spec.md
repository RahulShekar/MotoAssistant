Product Overview



Design a modern, premium, classic ios design, minimalistic mobile application called MotoOS.



MotoOS is an AI-powered motorcycle expedition companion designed for long-distance riders and adventure motorcyclists.



The app is NOT a social network, marketplace, booking platform, or trip planner.



MotoOS exists for one purpose:



"Help riders safely reach their destination by continuously monitoring navigation, fuel availability, weather conditions, road risks, network coverage, and rider readiness."



The experience should feel like having an intelligent riding companion sitting beside the rider throughout the journey.
